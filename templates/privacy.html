Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, username, email address, phone number, password
  • Resume Data: Work experience, education, skills, certifications, projects, and other professional information
  • Job Links: URLs, descriptions, and screenshots of job postings you share
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, features used, and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our resume building services
  • Generate tailored resumes based on job descriptions
  • Run ATS analysis and rejection prediction
  • Improve our AI algorithms and features
  • Send you important updates and notifications
  • Verify your account via OTP (email or SMS)

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
  • OTP codes expire after 10 minutes
  • Resume data is encrypted at rest
  • We use HTTPS for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings. Deleted data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our platform (hosting, analytics)
  • Law enforcement when required by law

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze traffic. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@hiredin.com
