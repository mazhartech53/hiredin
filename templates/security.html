Security & Data Privacy

Your security is our top priority

Account Security

  • Password Hashing: All passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt rounds before storage. We never store plain-text passwords.
  • Login Options: Login with email or username — simple and secure.
  • Session Management: Secure session cookies with HttpOnly and Secure flags.

Data Protection

  • Encryption at Rest: Resume data and personal information are encrypted in our database.
  • HTTPS Everywhere: All data transmission uses TLS 1.3 encryption.
  • No Data Selling: We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect what is necessary to provide our services.

Infrastructure Security

  • Secure Hosting: Our servers are hosted on secure, SOC 2 compliant infrastructure.
  • Regular Backups: Automated daily backups with encrypted storage.
  • DDoS Protection: Built-in protection against distributed denial-of-service attacks.
  • Vulnerability Scanning: Regular security audits and penetration testing.

Compliance

  • GDPR Ready: We respect your right to access, correct, and delete your data.
  • Data Retention: You can delete your account and all data at any time. We purge deleted data within 30 days.
  • Transparency: Clear privacy policy and cookie policy. No hidden tracking.

Security Tips for Users

  • Use a strong, unique password for your HiredIn account
  • Never share your login credentials
  • Log out from shared devices
  • Report suspicious activity through the Contact Us page

Security Contact

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly through our Contact Us page. We take all reports seriously and will respond within 48 hours.
