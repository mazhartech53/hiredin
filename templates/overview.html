About HiredIn

Our Mission

HiredIn was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a fair chance at their dream job, regardless of where they live or what profession they're in. We noticed that job seekers worldwide struggle with country-specific resume formats, ATS rejections, and lack of guidance on what recruiters actually want.

What We Do

HiredIn is an AI-powered resume platform that helps professionals build, analyze, and optimize resumes for any country and any job. Our platform supports:

  • 8+ Country Formats: USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, India, UAE — each with unique formatting rules
  • ATS Optimization: Beat the bots with keyword analysis and formatting checks
  • Job Link Matching: Paste any job description and get a tailored resume
  • Rejection Prediction: Fix issues before recruiters see them
  • Multi-Profession Support: Works for tech, healthcare, finance, education, marketing, sales, and more
  • Migration Mode: Moving countries? Convert your resume format instantly
