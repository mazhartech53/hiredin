How do I build a resume for a specific country?

Go to the Resume Builder, select your target country from the dropdown, and fill in your details. The form will automatically adapt to that country's requirements (e.g., photo for Germany/India, no photo for USA/UK).

What is the Job Link feature?

Job Link allows you to paste a job posting URL or description. Our system analyzes the requirements and helps you build a resume tailored specifically for that job role and country.

How does the ATS Checker work?

Paste your resume text into the ATS Checker. Our AI scans for keywords, formatting issues, action verbs, quantifiable achievements, and common mistakes that cause ATS systems to reject resumes. You get a score and actionable feedback.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bcrypt password hashing, HTTPS encryption, and secure sessions. Your resume data is encrypted at rest. We never sell your data. Read our full Security Policy.

Can I use HiredIn for any profession?

Yes! HiredIn works for all professions — tech, healthcare, finance, education, marketing, sales, operations, design, research, and more. Our AI adapts to your industry.

Is HiredIn really free?

Yes! During our launch phase, all features are completely free. We will notify you well in advance before introducing any paid plans. No credit card required.
